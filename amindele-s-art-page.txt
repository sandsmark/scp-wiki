Hey! Welcome to my art page. I'm going to use this as a repository for all the SCP drawings I've made. Some of this is pretty old, so it's not all great, but I wanted everything in one place. 
There's more I still need to put on here, but I'm going to have to go on a scavenger hunt for all the files, so it'll take a while.

(Psst - The Yaldabaoth drawing is probably the best one here. Go take a look at her!)
