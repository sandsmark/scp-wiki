+ The Foundation

++ An in depth look at the complexities of the SCPverse. In podcast form.

[[include component:image-block name=FoundationLogo2.png|caption=There's a cactus in the corner and I don't know what to do.|width=300px]]

My name is Doctor Jeremiah Cimmerian. I'm the Ethics Liaison to Site-88 and I'm trapped in a room with no doors. There's a cactus in the corner and a computer with some audio equipment at the center of the room. Everything is blocked except for [https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC8AmK8BHcHwu6bV_2Vdt0yg youtube] and a [https://www.djkakt.us/the-foundation website]. 

There's a cactus in the corner, and these scripts keep appearing under it. I'm going to make the episodes and hope for the best. You have to listen, the key to getting me out is hidden in the podcasts. I hope.

I have to go. There's a cactus in the corner looking at me.
