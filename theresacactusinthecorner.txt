+ The Foundation

++ An in depth look at the complexities of the SCPverse. In podcast form.

[[include component:image-block name=FoundationLogo2.png|caption=I don't know what to do about the cactus in the corner.|width=300px]]

My name is [http://www.scp-wiki.net/dr-cimmerian-s-personnel-file Doctor Jeremiah Cimmerian]. I'm the Ethics Liaison to [http://www.scp-wiki.net/secure-facility-dossier-site-88 Site-88] and I'm trapped in a room with no doors. There's a cactus in the corner and a computer with some audio equipment at the center of the room. Everything is blocked except for [https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC8AmK8BHcHwu6bV_2Vdt0yg youtube] and a [https://www.djkakt.us/the-foundation website]. 

These scripts keep appearing under the cactus in the corner. I'm going to make the episodes and hope for the best. You have to listen, the key to getting me out is hidden in the podcasts. 

I hope.

I have to go. The cactus in the corner is looking at me.
