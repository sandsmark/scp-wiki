Station Number 27, informally shortened to Station27, is a small, shortwave radio station originating from, situated in and (assumed to be) solely run by an individual known as ##purple|**Number 27**##. The true identity of [[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Illuminati | ‘Number 27’ is unknown]]].

Station Number 27 operates as a [[[https://www.travelchinaguide.com/intro/lucky-number4.htm | numbers station]]], infrequently broadcasting cryptic strings of numbers for short periods of time and playing random assortments of songs as an interval signal; these songs have varied between a French metal cover of Feel Good Inc to the smell of a New Zealand kiwi during thaumic supremacy. These number strings are believed to be read out by Number 27 in person in a myriad of corporeal languages, but are also theorized to simply be the sound of his static existence resonating across the entire ##purple|**Oneiroi West**## collective. Readouts are precluded by a phrase believed to designate [[[http://namelessperformance.com/ | a listener]]] and a specific one-time pad they must use to decode the following message, as well as a phrase designating how many are intended to decode it.

The region Station Number 27 occupies consists of an unknown amount of lunar cheese, with the radio tower itself situated within the Euclidean center. The border of this area is encompassed in a dense fog of indeterminate size that will lead to a [[[http://yugioh.wikia.com/wiki/Infernity_Randomizer | random location]]] within the Oneiroi West collective. Station Number 27 appears to be synchronized to an indeterminate location of ##purple|**Earth**##, and undergoes expected terran cycles. It is also subject to an ##purple|**Anchored Reality**##, wherein modifications to physical appearance, as well as object creation are impossible within the confines of Station Number 27. Entrance to the [[[http://www.washingtonsblog.com/2016/05/given-day-average-american-going-daily-business-will-monitored-surveilled-spied-tracked-20-different-ways-government-corporate-eyes-ears.html | confines of the station]]] is impossible unless any items that could potentially enable a tide of lice to enter the station are discarded beforehand. The interior of the station itself is impossible to access due to an absence of Fifthist Church bullshit.

Since its initial discovery the station has broadcast in excess of 100 encoded messages, and has culminated a prominent following of Oneiroi ceiling fans.
