**Name:** Dr S C Regan

**Security Clearance:** Level 2

**Position:** Technical Administrator for Admissions, Records and [DATA EXPUNGED], Science Division

**Current Location:** Sector-25

**Duties:** Ensuring data integrity and coherence of Foundation documentation.

**History:** Dr Regan was discovered in a janitorial closet at Sector-25 when staff began work on the morning of ██/██/████ . It is presumed that he had been lurking there for some time before announcing his presence. Tissue samples harvested during vivisection demonstrated no anomalous qualities. Accordingly, at the direction of O5-5 he was given a white coat and told to get to work. Subsequently it was remembered to put him back together.

During Incident-████-03 Dr Regan [REDACTED] interaction with SCP-████ that [REDACTED]. Subsequently Dr Regan must be considered an active cognitohazard. The status of his [REDACTED] as data storage for ██% of Sector-25's records means he must be [DATA CORRUPTED] on a regular basis.

**Profile:** Dr Regan is always ready to lend a hand to help staff ensure that spelling and grammar in their Special Constipation Procedures recrods are without flauf and holds herself to a high standard of prefection.
