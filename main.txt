[[div class="news-title"]]
August 30, 2017 
[[/div]]
[[div class="news-content"]]

The **[http://wanderers-library.wikidot.com Wanderers' Library]** and **[http://visual-records.wikidot.com Visual Records]** Sites have both received significant overhauls! The Library has been tweaking its content presentation for a few months now and has been given a fresh coat of paint! The Visual Records wiki has been scrubbed of all images which were not compliant with the CC-by-SA 3.0 License and can now fulfill its potential as a SCP Fuel and Artwork repository! It has also received a fresh coat of paint! Check 'em out!

[[/div]]

[[div class="news-content"]]

To increase voting and readership for under-reviewed articles, a new site project has been started: the [http://www.scp-wiki.net/young-and-under-30 "Young and under 30"] project is aiming to get more attention to articles less than a year old, and with a score of less than +30. Come check it out and participate!
