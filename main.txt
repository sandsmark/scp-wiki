[[div class="content-panel centered standalone"]]
[[span style="font-size: 200%; line-height: 100%;"]]
Ladies and gentlemen, mark your calendars: the [[[http://www.scp-wiki.net/144-hour-jam-contest |144-Hour Jam Contest]]] has begun! 
[[/span]]

It runs from March 9 until March 15. You will be challenged to write three separate articles in less than 48 hours apiece. Compete for honor, glory, and meaningless prizes!
[[/div]]

[[div class="content-panel centered standalone"]]
Come one, come all, to the first ever [http://scp-int.wikidot.com/international-goi-contest-2019 SCP-International contest]!

In this contest, users from around the globe will compete to write an article featuring a Group of Interest... from another branch! Will you pick -FR's Order of Light? -JP's Drunkeness Street? With so many branches and so many groups, you may literally drown in inspiration!

Follow [http://scp-int.wikidot.com/international-goi-contest-2019 this link right here, these exact words,] to the contest page - you'll find the rules there, and remember to represent -EN (that's us) with good writing and better sportsmanship!
[[/div]]
