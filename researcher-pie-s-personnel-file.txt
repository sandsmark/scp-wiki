[[collapsible show="scp-4460" hide="scp-4460"]]
So, not exactly sure where on Earth I got this idea. All I know know is that I hate the article and it is bad.
Yeah that's it
[[/collapsible]]
[[collapsible show="scp-4944" hide="scp-4944"]]
I initially got the idea while in the car with my mom. She mentioned that our coffee machine sounds like a fog horn while its heating up. The idea instantly popped into my head: "A coffee machine that if you press a certain button, it will turn into a cargo ship"

That obviously isn't what happened, though.
I honestly don't even know how I got the idea that turned out to be the final product. All I knew was that I was taking my coffee machine out into my back yard, barefoot, right after it snowed. Yeah, it was cold.

After some crooping and cropping, I got all the text to sound right in my head.

Apparently people really like this scip. It's currently my most upvoted article, and even got into an episode of SCP Cafè, which is a level of loft I will never reach again.

All in all, I still like this article.
[[/collapsible]]
