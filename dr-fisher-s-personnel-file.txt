**Name:** Dr. ██████ █████ Fisher

**Security Clearance Level:** Level 3

**Occupation:** SCP Researcher, Theorist

**Site of Operations:** Dr. Fisher's office is located within Research Sector-15.  Contact via PDA suggested.

**History:** Submitted application and resume after assisting Foundation operatives during [[DATA EXPUNGED]].  Hired 2 years later, ██/██/19██.  Dr. Fisher is a PhD in Mathematics and a Bachelor in both Philosophy and Biology.  His duties within the Foundation and Research Sector-15 involve SCP application, destruction, and ████████████.

Since [[DATA EXPUNGED]], Dr. Fisher has displayed empathy toward inanimate objects.  This empathy usually manifests itself in Dr. Fisher scolding his equipment or carrying one-sided conversations with furniture. Whether this behavior is the result of [[DATA EXPUNGED]] or SCP exposure is being investigated. When interacting with inanimate objects capable of sentient thought and response (SCPs and AI), Dr. Fisher displays signs of irritation.
