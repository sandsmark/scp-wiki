[[tab So, You Want to Add to The Page?]]

To add to this page, DM Karakatt via [http://www.wikidot.com/user:info/karakatt Wikidot,] [https://discord.com/ Discord (Karakatt#9875),] or [http://www.scp-wiki.net/chat-guide IRC (Karakatt/Karakatt132).] To have your art added, you must:

* You must be able to prove it is **your** art, 

* provide an Imgur link or the original .png/.jpg,

* Tell which scene, SCP, or tale it is from, 

* Say which tab it goes under,

* Give me your Wikidot username.

You must have all of the above for your art to be submitted to the page. 

**Kara reserves the right to refuse any submission for any reason.**
