**Item #:** SCP-173 

**Object class:** Euclid 

**Special Containment Procedures:** Item SCP-173 is to be kept in a locked container at all times. When personnel must approach SCP-173's island, no fewer than 3 may enter at any time. At all times, two persons must maintain direct eye contact with SCP-173 until all personnel have vacated and relocked the container. 

**Description:** Moved to Site19 1993. Origin is as of yet unknown. It is constructed from copper and rebar with traces of gold leaf. SCP-173 is animate and extremely hostile. The object cannot move while within a direct line of sight. Line of sight must not be broken at any time with SCP-173. Personnel assigned to enter container are instructed to alert one another before blinking. Object is reported to attack by crushing the subject under its feet, or by immolation. In the event of an attack, personnel are to observe Class 4 hazardous object containment procedures.
