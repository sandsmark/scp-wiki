+++ Editorials' Picks

[[collapsible show="+ Click to Reveal!" hide="- Click to Close"]]
++++ Uncle Nicolini

[[*user T Rutherford]] has had a very strong premiere as a new author. I highly recommend reading their work, which is available [[[t-rutherford-author-page|here.]]] Keep an eye on them heading into 2020!

[[*user Woedenaz]] has developed a tasteful collection of themes that use impressive CSS and design skills. They are viewable [[[woedenaz/offset/1|here.]]]

And last but not least, here is my favorite art piece from last year:

= [[image http://scp-wiki.wdfiles.com/local--files/scp-952/HighwayAsh.png style="width:50%"]]
Made by [[*user Woedenaz]] for [[*user uraniumempire]] for the 2019 Art Exchange. It's a beautiful rendition of the album in Uranium's [[[SCP-952]]]!
-----
++++ UraniumEmpire

[[*user Tsercele]] started off with [[[SCP-4581]]] and hit the g-ddamn ground running! Keep an eye on their hit series, [[[The Eternal MxTape]]].

The best skip of the year, in my humble opinion, was [[[SCP-4504]]]. If you've got some time, give it a read!
-----
++++ TheMightyMcB
[[*user kybard]] showed up out of the blue and just started kicking ass and taking names! Be sure to check him out at [[[http://www.scp-wiki.net/kybard|his author page]]] and read all three of his skips, because they're excellent.

One of my favorite skips from 2019, and one that I find to be massively underrated, was [[[SCP-3638]]] "You Can't Be Serious!", by [[*user 440 Hertz]]. It even has a really fun collaborative art page!
-----
++++ gee0765

I recommend [[[SCP-4338]]], [[[SCP-3272]]], [[[SCP-4368]]], and [[[SCP-4357]]].

My favorite breakout author of the year was none other than [[*user T Rutherford]]!
-----
++++ Vincent Van Gone

My favorite SCP of the year was [[[SCP-4390]]], and [[*user Tanhony]] really popped off this year!
-----
++++ Croquembouche

I highly recommend you check out the End of Death subseries, [[[the-existential-maw-of-despair-national-park|Lookout 281.]]]

++++ A Random Day

MUCKSCAPE/MEGAGRID/MAYHEM series (http://www.scp-wiki.net/muckscape-megagrid-mayhem-hub); Maim The Sky, Slay the Sun series (http://www.scp-wiki.net/maim-the-sky-slay-the-sun-hub) are my picks.

There was exactly one author i can remember who wrote good-ass shit last year and his name is [[*user pedantique]].
-----
++++ MalyceGraves

[[[SCP-4140]]], [[[Breakneck]]], [[[SCP-4840]]], [[[SCP-4372]]] and the new author [[*user Hatcheye]] are my picks.
-----
++++ Elogee FishTruck

Some great content got made for the International Group of Interest competition! I'd recommend you all check out the [[[sky-sermon-hub|Sky Sermon series]]], [[[non-prophet-organization-hub|A Non-Prophet Organization series]]], and [[[SCP-4775]]] and [[[SCP-4642]]] for the Meat Circus GoI!

+* Q&A:

++* Q: Can I replace a blurb that you wrote for one of my articles with my own? I'm not satisfied with it.

A: Absolutely. You will always have that option at any point.

++* Q: I noticed an error/typo/incorrect information and wish to correct it. May I?

A: Sure! If the changes are not limited to your own article(s) and/or are substantial changes across multiple entries, you should post in that month's discussion page and then make the changes.

++* Q: I wrote an article and it seems to have stuck. Can I send a blurb of mine to one of the frequent collaborators?

A: Yup! It's much easier if you come to us about it, and it gives you the control over what you want shown on the news page.
