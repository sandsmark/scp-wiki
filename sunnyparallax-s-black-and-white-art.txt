> The following is a collection of SunnyParallax's art work, mostly featuring more uncommonly drawn SCPs. Pieces are drafted and drawn by hand, and colored using a computer afterwards. All personnel can make art requests [http://www.scp-wiki.net/forum/t-861433/taking-art-requests here] if interested. This page will continue to be updated.
