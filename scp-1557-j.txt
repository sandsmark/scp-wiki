<span class="bold">Item #:</span> SCP-1557-J

<br><br>

<span class="bold">Object Class:</span> Safe for children ages 3 and up.

<br><br>

<span class="bold">Special Containment Procedures:</span>
SCP-1557-J is to remain in a queue for researchers to use when writing informational articles. SCP-1557-J is to be thoroughly disinfected prior to each transfer of possession. Access Level 0 is needed to handle SCP-1557-J. Under no circumstances are any personnel to bite down with pressure exceeding 20lbs to prevent choking hazards.

<br><br>

<span class="bold">Queue #:</span> <span id="queue-number"></span>

<br><br>

<span class="bold">Description:</span>
SCP-1557-J is an abnormally shaped wooden block approximately 6x6x6 centimeters large. Most of SCP-1557-J's surface is covered in human tooth marks.<sup class="tooltip">?</sup><div class="tooltip-text">Note made August 3rd, 2019: Due to having a medium-sized chunk bitten out of it, the object class has been changed from "Safe" to "Safe for children ages 3 and up".<br><br> - Dr. Kaiza</div>

<br><br>

<h1 class="flash-red">Important Announcement</h1>
<h3>Audio Log Transcribed</h3>
<div class="blockquote">
<strong>&lt;Begin Log&gt;</strong><br><br>
<i>The sound of intense throat-clearing blares over the loudspeakers as they suddenly turn on.</i><br><br>
<i>A faint, nervous, voice in the background makes its appearance.</i> Uhm, sir. The microphone is on.<br><br>
Oh! <i>Startled, an official-sounding voice begins to speak.</i> Uh, good morning Site-██.<br><br>
<i>There is a brief pause, along with a slight sniffle and a sigh. The official-sounding voice returns, it seems as though it is holding back tears.</i><br><br>
I am sad to announce the death of Junior Researcher Tanny. He was truly a saint as well as a keyboard demon in the moments leading up to his death. He was, in fact, the best of both worlds. Being "funny", "cool", and "cray-cray" were just a small fraction of the things that he was known for.<br><br>
If only his--uh, his um, hold on a sec'. <i>A loud clack radiates from the loudspeakers. The sound of wood.</i><br><br>
If only his short life wasn't just a slight bit longer, we might have realized what was wrong. The splinter we retrieved from his throat during the autopsy has been ceremoniously tossed into the incinerator in order to power our facility so that <strong>even in death</strong> his memory can empower us all to be the most productive in writing up our reports as we could ever be.<br><br>
This has been a sad farewell. Dr. Kaiza out.<br><br>
<strong>&lt;End Log&gt;</strong>
</div>

function rand(min, max) {
  min = Math.ceil(min);
  max = Math.floor(max);
  return Math.floor(Math.random() * (max - min + 1)) + min;
}
