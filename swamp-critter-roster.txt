+ Meet the Swamp Critters!


The Swamp Critters are a group of individuals (staff, veteran authors, longtime experienced reviewers) dedicated to leaving helpful critique on final works posted to the main site. Their goal is to cultivate and curate an environment of constructive criticism on the wiki.

Though we all are experts on the basics for what makes a successful site author, we each have our own specializations and skillsets regarding the different kinds of writing you see on the wiki. Using this page, you will hopefully be able to contact a critter who is best suited for your particular style.


+ GUIDELINES FOR CONTACTING US

* ##blue|//Please include a link to your article.//##

* Please make sure to read through our individual profiles to find the best critter for you.

* If someone doesn't reply promptly, please don't spam their inbox asking why they haven't replied. A lot of us have busy schedules off the site, so if you've been waiting more than a few days for a response, feel free to reach out to another critter.

* Remember, all pages posted to the main site must be complete and final work. Don't ask us to review a draft!

* Site Crit members may decline to review material when the basics[[footnote]]Generally correct grammar, mechanics, formatting[[/footnote]] are not met, or for other reasons (such as not being interested in the article or having real-life commitments/time constraints).

* Please be polite! (We're unpaid volunteers here.)
