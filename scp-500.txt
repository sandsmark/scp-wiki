**Item #:** SCP-500

**Object Class:** Safe

**Special Containment Procedures:**

SCP-500 must be stored in a cool and dry place away from bright light. SCP-500 is only allowed to be accessed by personnel with level 4 security clearance to prevent misapplication.

**Description:** 

SCP-500 is a small plastic can which at the time of writing contains fifty-nine (59) red pills. One pill, when taken orally, effectively cures the subject of all diseases within two hours, exact time depending on the severity and amount of the subject's conditions. Despite extensive trials, all attempts at synthesizing more of what is thought to be the active ingredient of SCP-500 have been unsuccessful.

> **Note From Dr. Klein:**
> SCP personal below Level 3 are now banned from handling SCP-500. This is not to be used to cure a hangover. Get AIDS and then ask permission.

**Addendum 500-1:** Two (2) pills have been authorized for use with [[[SCP-008]]]. As a result of conducting a series of tests on Class D subjects infected with SCP-008, it appears that even in the most advanced stages of the disease one whole pill will accomplish full recovery. Number of pills are fifty-seven (57) at the time of writing. - Dr. [500-0021D]

**Addendum 500-2:** One (1) pill has been authorized for use with [[[SCP-409]]]. SCP-500 was tested on Subject 409-D5 who was exposed to the effects of [[[SCP-409]]].  Complete recovery accomplished. See Addendum 409-1. Number of pills are fifty-six (56) at the time of writing. - Dr. [500-0021D]

**Addendum 500-3:** One (1) pill has been authorized for to test SCP-500's effectiveness on [[[SCP-107]]]. Personnel D-4█████ was exposed to [[[SCP-107]]], then to SCP-500 (after reaching comatose state, to test full effectiveness). Subject immediately stopped "hearing" [[[SCP-107]]]. Subject was re-exposed to former host body of [[[SCP-107]]]. No results. [[[SCP-107]]] reclassified as Neutralized. Number of pills are fifty-five (55) at the time of writing.

**Addendum 500-4:** Request 500-1774-e approved. Five (5) pills have been used for experiments with [[[SCP-038]]]. SCP-038 can duplicate SCP-500. However, success rates are low, and generally leave the victim with lingering effects if death is averted. However, for all personnel dosing with SCP-038 cloned pills is recommended, as supply of SCP-500 remains extremely limited. All five (5) used samples of SCP-500 were returned. Number of pills are fifty-five (55) at the time of writing.

**Addendum 500-5:** During experiments with SCP-038, one (1) pill was stolen by personnel D-██████ to, reportedly, "cure a hangover". Stricter controls for samples of SCP-500 given to other projects is suggested. Personnel D-██████ has been terminated. Number of pills are fifty-four (54) at the time of writing.

**Addendum 500-5:** One (1) pill has been used with [[[SCP-041]]] to cure SCP-107 infestation. Number of pills are fifty-three (53) at the time of writing.

**Addendum 500-6:** One (1) pill has been used with [[[SCP-231|SCP-231-4]]]. Number of pills are fifty-two (52) at the time of writing.

**Addendum 500-7**: One (1) pill has been used for [[[ experiment-log-447-a | Experiment 447-a]]]. Number of pills are fifty-one (51) at the time of writing.

**Addendum 500-8:** One (1) pill has been used with [[[SCP-208]]]. Number of pills are fifty (50) at the time of writing.
