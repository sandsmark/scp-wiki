**Item #:** @@SCP-9863-J@@
 
**Object Class:** @@--unknown-- unsafe@@
 
**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-9863-J must be kept in a secure vault fitting the suitcases size around 1x0.5 and shall not be removed.
 
**Description:** SCP-9863-J is a large suitcase with three stamps. It is unknown if the stamps are the cause or the suitcase. The suitcases or stamps affect are that anyone carrying it will be involved in an horrible accident. The carrier only lands in an accident if he/she is travelling. As the state of it is it is known that the carrier will survive but get badly injured.
