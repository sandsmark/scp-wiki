**About:**

[[collapsible show="+ click to read alternate information presentation to help you take me seriously" hide="- why the fuck would you want to take me seriously though."]]

Anyway, I'm Cyan. My username means cyan and chartreuse. (It was originally just going to be Chartreuse, but that was taken on Wikidot. Ultimately I'm glad I went with this because "Char" would have been an odd nickname as opposed to Cyan.) Specifically #00ffff and #ceff00 when it comes to my graphic design stuff. Did you know that there are quite a few people who remember chartreuse as red or deep magenta rather than yellow-green, and that [http://mandelaeffect.com/chartreuse-red-or-green/ some believe] that and phenomena similar to it might potentially prove that alternate realities are more than science fiction?

So, I'm actually pretty boring. I just hide how boring I am with false illusions of dank memes and general stupidity. I've been in the SCP fandom since early 2014 and it's one of my favorite things in the world. If you're active on the Tumblr end of the fandom, you know me as [http://sexycontainmentprocedures.tumblr.com/ sexycontainmentprocedures] (I post all my Photoshop and cosplay there, make terrible shitposts, and generally be a nuisance, and yet somehow I get followers.) I support goofy Foundationverse canon and 2srs Foundationverse canon in equal parts, and it shows in my writing; there's room for all of our headcanons here on the site, and they can overlap in wonderful ways.

I work in the film industry as of late, which was a welcome change from my boring retail jobs. I legit used to sell dildos for a living at one of Adam & Eve's competitor retailers, I shit you not. That shit only paid 7.50 an hour and was totally not worth the customer interaction. Life's wild bro.

Anyway, please feel free to reach out to me about anything, particularly cosplay and graphic design (the stuff I'm actually good at, unlike literally anything else). I'm pretty chill and down to talk to anyone, no worries.
