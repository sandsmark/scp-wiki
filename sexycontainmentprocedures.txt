**Name:** Agent Cyrus Trauss

**Personnel Class:** C

**Clearance Level:** 4

**Department(s):** Offsite Response (current), Field Operations (current), Security (secondary), MTF λ12 (former)

**Site(s) Assigned:** 42 (current), 19, 81, 66 (former)

**Hire Date:** ██/██/2016

**Personnel Information:** C-51174 is primarily tasked with tracking and detaining human and sapient anomalies in the southeastern states, responding to local emergencies, and responding to civilian calls in Wilmington, NC; if necessary, he can be assigned to onsite security or containment positions. He lives on-site at Site-42 and is unmarried with no children.
