**Security Clearance Level:** 3

**Specialties:** Robotics, Automation, Aviation

**Location:** Toronto, Ontario. May be located at other facilities or places as required.

**Description:** EngTech Xiao is a Foundation engineering specialist, who was employed after an incident involving an unrelated containment breach that was contained with assistance provided by him. EngTech Xiao is responsible for categorizing electro-mechanical SCPs, while studying such SCPs and applying that knowledge to Foundation-based projects.

At the moment, EngTech Xiao is currently in Toronto, working with his company on project FD-003, while maintaining cover by producing civilian and commercial robotics for the community.
