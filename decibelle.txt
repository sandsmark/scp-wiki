+ Rose Labelle

[[tabview]]
[[tab Information]]
**Name:** [http://www.scp-wiki.net/system:page-tags/tag/researcher-labelle#pages Rose Labelle]

**D.O.B.:** 09/13/1980

**Security Clearance:** Level 2 (Restricted)

**Position:** Programmer, IT Specialist, Researcher of Anomalous Programs & Computer Software

**Sites:** 19

**Profile:** Rose Labelle, was employed by the Foundation in 2009 following activation of SCP-████ at [REDACTED]. Since then, Rose Labelle has maintained a low profile.

Any rumors spread by others pertaining to [[[this-is-the-real-life|anomalous expertise and feats]]] surrounding Researcher Labelle are to be discredited and reported to her immediately for investigation.

//Seriously, if I find out who's been writing about these, I'm going to talk with your supervisor. The next round of these to show up in my email are being filtered into the trash. ~ Researcher Rose Labelle//
