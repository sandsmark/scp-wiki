**Item #:** SCP-1294

**Object Class:** Safe/Euclid

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-1294 is to be secured in a one by one by one meter (1x1x1) titanium-alloyed safe, guarded by D-Class personnel. The watch is to be taken out of its containment biweekly to be serviced by agents with a clearance level of 2 or higher. Anyone seen leaving the containment facility with SCP-1294 will be terminated.
