**Lafyyi #:** WAV-1294
 
**I'puyaf Airaeww:** Ygairlak
 
**Wvyalaaeir Ai'lfaelalyiylf Vuai'aykguayw:** WAV-1294 law fi' py ai'lfaelalyk lal ae uai'i'yi yiyaewgualalj 5 yi de' 5 yi de' 3 yi, iolafo ae slairfyuayk phylflairaeflai'l wilwfyyi. Vuai'vyua yiyklaaaeir saealairlaflayw aeuay fi' py yiaelalfaelalyk aef aeirir flayiyw, aelk i'ly zlairi'juaaeyi i's uaaeio yiyaef woi'girk py vuai'phlakyk i'lay aef 8:00 Ae.Yi.
 
Lalfyuaaeaflai'l pyfioyyl wfaess aelk WAV-1294 law fi' py zyvf fi' ae yilallayigyi. WAV-1294 syyklalj, juai'i'yilalj, aelk ioaewfy klawvi'waeir oaephy pyyl aegfi'yiaefyk, aelk aeirir vyuawi'llyir yigwf ioyaeua ae uaywvlauaaefi'ua pysi'uay ylfyualalj WAV-1294'w ai'lfaelalyiylf aoaeyipyua.

Yii'plairy Faewz Si'uaay De'la-8 (aezae "Wvyaeuaoglfyuaw") aeuay fi' py kyviri'ilyk fi' foy Waeoaeuaae Kywyuaf lal wyaeuaao i's sguafoyua lalwfaelayw i's WAV-1294 glkyua foy jglawy i's vi'aeaoyuaw. YiFS De'la-8  aeuay fi' fuaaelwvi'uaf lalwfaelayw fi' Wlafy ██ si'ua fyuayilalaeflai'l phlaae lalalalyuaaeflai'l.
 
**Kywaualavflai'l:** WAV-1294 aevvyaeuaw fi' py ae yiaeiry syllya si'de' (//Vulpes zerda//) yiyaewgualalj 27 ayi lal iryljfo, aelk ioylajolalj aevvuai'de'layiaefyiril 1.1 zlairi'juaaeyiw aef yilallayigyi. Paeuaualalj oglflalj yiyfoi'kw aelk vuai'auayaeflai'l, WAV-1294 oaew woi'iol li' klassyuaylayw lal pyoaephlai'ua foael foaef i's ae filvlaaaeir syllya si'de'.

WAV-1294 law ai'yivi'wyk ylflauayiril i's ae sgljaeir yiaefyualaaeir, ioolaao uayvirlaaaefyw lafw sgua, wzyiryfi'l, aelk sgua. Foy yiaeui'ualafil i's WAV-1294'w lalfyualaeir wfuagafguay ai'lwlawfw i's ae folaaz yii'irk i's phaeualalj phlawai'wlafil. WAV-1294 ai'lflalgi'gwiril yde'oaeiryw ae fi'de'lal, ai'lfaelallalj oyaephil aeyii'glfw i's vwlairi'ailplal aelk yigwalayii'ir.

Las irysf yde'vi'wyk si'ua iri'ljyua foael aevvuai'de'layiaefyiril fioi' yilalgfyw, wgpuyafw iolairir ylfyua ae ai'lwlawfylfiril lalauayaewlalj wfaefy i's ygvoi'ualaae glflair uayyii'phyk suai'yi WAV-1294'w vuaywylay. Wgpuyafw iolairir pyai'yiy pualaysiril klawfuaywwyk las uayyii'phyk suai'yi WAV-1294, pgf li' vyuayiaelylf i'ua iri'lj-iraewflalj yssyafw oaephy pyyl si'glk.

Wgpuyafw yde'vi'wyk si'ua iri'ljyua foael slaphy yilalgfyw, oaephy woi'iol fi' py olajoiril uaywlawfaelf fi' vaelal, aelk pyoaephy lal ae wlayilairaeua yiaellyua fi' foi'wy glkyua foy lalsirgylay i's ae vwilaoykyirlaa oaeirirgalali'jyl. Wgpuyafw uayyii'phyk suai'yi WAV-1294 oaephy li'f uayai'phyuayk suai'yi foy yiaeui'ualafil i's foywy yssyafw, aelk oaephy pyyl zli'iol fi' lallaflaaefy wyirs-oaeuayi lal ael aeffyyivf fi' vuai'phi'zy wflayigirla.

Las WAV-1294 wyyw ae wgpuyaf lal folaw wfaefy, WAV-1294 iolairir irlay i'l, i'ua aejaelalwf foy wgpuyaf, aelk uaywf. Kgualalj folaw flayiy, WAV-1294'w sgua iolairir aevvyaeua fi' yiyirf, aelk juai'io i'phyua foy wgpuyaf, iraefyua wolasflalj laf lalfi' WAV-1294'w aepki'yiyl. WAV-1294 oaew woi'iol li' irlayilaf fi' iooaef laf aael ylphyiri'v, aelk law aaevaepiry i's vuai'aywwlalj i'phyua 190 yiyfualaa fi'lw i's si'i'k lal i'ly ioyyz. WAV-1294 i'liril ai'lwgyiyw irlaphlalj vuayil lal folaw yiaellyua, vuai'ssyualalj fi' yaef aeiruayaekil kyaek wgpuyafw i'ua vuai'aywwyk si'i'k li'uayiaeiriril.
