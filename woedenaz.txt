* Hello! It somewhat befuddles me that anyone would click this. But if you truly want to know more about me, this is the place! Too bad I am absolutely awful at writing about myself. But still, I'll give some things... _
 _
I am a graphic designer for a living. I've made art and wrote for as long as I can remember. _
 _
I'm also gay and a dude, if that's something you care about. Came out really late in life, so that factors in to my writing quite a bit. _
 _
I really like writing stories that have mysteries to unpack. Like puzzles for the reader to find. I try to leave in little clues rather than just outright telling people. _
 _
Okay, that's all you get. Thanks for stopping by!
