+ Dr. Lucius Veritas

**Serial Number:** 3475-83537

**Clearance Level:** 3

**Position:** Senior Researcher/containment specialist of [[[http://www.scp-wiki.net/scp-3550 |SCP-3550]]].

**Site of Operations:** 42

**Profile:** Specialised in personnel/humanoid interaction and containment procedures, but inexperienced in other fields, Veritas' primary service is continued containment of humanoid SCP objects and is currently one of the overseeing researchers mandating testing on [[[http://www.scp-wiki.net/scp-914 |SCP-914]]].

> **FROM:** Dr. Daniels, Junior Researcher, Site 42
> **TO:** Dr. Gears, [REDACTED]
> **SUBJECT:** Testing with 914
> 
> Gears, can you get Veritas off my back? I promise to be careful this time, I even read and signed his stupid security manual for 914 three times!
> 
> ~ Junior Researcher Daniels.

> **FROM:** Dr. Gears, [REDACTED]
> **TO:** Dr. Daniels, Junior Researcher, Site 42
> **SUBJECT:** RE: Testing with 914
> 
> Dear Dr. Daniels,
> 
> According to the testing logs from two weeks ago, you attempted to 'refine' a flamethrower, which resulted in considerable damage to SCP-914's containment chamber and SCP-914 itself. The repairs are still being made at the time of writing. I was not present at the time of testing, but at least two researchers described the result as, and I quote: ''Something you would see in a Terminator movie''. While I do not have experience with the particular film, Dr. Julie is still in therapy following the incident. I feel forced to side with Dr. Veritas on this one.
> 
> ~ Dr. Gears.
