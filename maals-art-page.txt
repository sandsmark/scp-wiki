Hi, I'm Mal/Maal! After some coercion it seems as though I've finally come around to putting my art up in some official capacity. I've been drawing SCP-related art for the last few years now, so I've chosen just a few to share with the site that inspired me so much for so long. My art has been featured on here before; recently a [http://securecontainplsno.tumblr.com/ blog I co-run] was mentioned on the [[[news-11-2016|monthly news]]] (I'm Mod Pink), but all that stuff is silly, and I don't spend too much time on it.
