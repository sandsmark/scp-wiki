**Name:** Izumi K██████ ("Enma Ai")

------------------------------

**Security Clearance:** Level 1

**Occupation:** Undercover agent

**Duties:** Information retrieval, Low-threat SCP acquisition

**Location:** Site-██

**History:** [DATA EXPUNGED], and after getting her limbs reattached via [REDACTED], was offered employment by the Foundation as an undercover agent, in light of her ability to remain incognito for long periods of time.

**Profile:** ██████ is very quiet and reclusive, and hardly responds to external events that do not immediately concerns her, which often results in various injuries as she fails to notice, for example, incoming projectiles that were not meant to hit her.

██████ affirms that her ability to remain unnoticeable come from years of "trying [her] hardest to never be noticed by people around [her]", an objective that she affirms to have achieved with a great degree of success. This, combined with her silent nature, often results in her being locked inside one of Site-██ rooms because no one noticed her presence.

When not deployed to the field, ██████ usually stays at her office, or cares for Site-██ gardens.
