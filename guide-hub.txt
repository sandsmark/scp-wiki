If you're looking for answers or advice for the SCP wiki, you should be able to find both of them here. If you have an idea for an informational page that you'd like to see added to this list, please [[[Contact Staff | contact a staff member]]].

[[size 150%]]
What are Guides and Essays? 
[[/size]]
* A ##38BBD7|Guide## is a staff-sanctioned informational page intended to assist community members with contributing to the site and its affiliates.
 * The “[http://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/system:page-tags/tag/guide guide]” navigational tag is only assigned by staff, or with staff permission.
* An ##43D738|Essay## is an informational page written about a specific topic, based on the author’s personal opinion. Essays also include “how to” resources as shared by authors who specialize in different aspects of site contribution (writing, coding, artwork, etc.).
 * The “[http://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/system:page-tags/tag/essay essay]” tag can be added by the author of the essay on their own, though it is advised to confirm with a staff member first.
