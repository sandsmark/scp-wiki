**Item #:** SCP-794

**Object Class:** Safe

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-711 is to be kept in vault ██, box 1█. Proper precautions must be taken upon opening the containment box. It must be opened in a facility under -10°C (negative ten degrees Celsius). No items made of gold or gold-like substances may be within 10 (ten) meters of said object.

**Description:** SCP-711 was locaten on ██/█/██ when Dr.██████


SCP-794 was discovered on ██/█/██, after reports of a ██████ Air Force helicopter disappearing during a flight through [REDACTED], as well as a second aircraft tasked to locate it. SCP-794 was located shortly thereafter by a Foundation search team, and current containment protocols established.

Current research is focused on determining the method by which SCP-794 came to rest at its current location, and determining the exact source of its anomalous properties.
