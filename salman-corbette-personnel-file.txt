**Name:** Research Assistant Salman "Fish Car" Corbette

**Security Clearance:** 2

**Occupation:** General Research

**Current Location:** Office set to be built under Site-19 expansion

**History:** Originally recruited from ██████████ University following reports of unusual modifications to vehicles, electronic devices, and candies. Although recruited with the intent of placement in engineering, Research Assistant Corbette expressed extreme disinterest, stating "It ain't fun anymore". Relocated to Site-19 and placed into research staff.
