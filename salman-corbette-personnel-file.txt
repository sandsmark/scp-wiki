**Name:** Research Assistant Salman "Fish Car" Corbette

**Security Clearance:** 2

**Occupation:** General Research

**Current Location:** Office set to be built under Site-19 expansion

**History:** Originally discovered by Foundation personnel driving an "awesome" vehicle, Research Assistant Corbette had been fleeing from GOC agents following his participation in [DATA EXPUNGED]. Although initially held for interrogation, Research Assistant Corbette impressed personnel with his intelligence; he divulged information to personnel without incident. Following interrogation Research Assistant Corbette was placed on staff.
