Here you'll find some articles and tales that I've personally really enjoyed. Keep in mind, this isn't a list designed to introduce new community members! Shoutout to [[*user Shaggydredlocks]] for putting together the awesome [[[User-Curated Lists]]], which functions far better in that regard.

That being said, if you have an article that you think deserves to be on this list, PM me! I'd love to read it.

**Other Important Note:** If you see an item on this list that is attributed to the wrong author because the Discord bot I used screwed up, please contact me about it and I'll correct it as soon as possible.
