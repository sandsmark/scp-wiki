**Item #:** SCP-XXXX

**Object Class:** Safe

**Special Containment Procedures:** --SCP-XXXX is kept in a standard storage unit in Site K-40. All SCP-XXXX-1 instances are to be kept in an adjacent unit, and new instances are only to be created for testing and must be recorded.-- 
SCP-XXXX is currently uncontained, for details see Addendum XXXX-A.

**Description:** SCP-XXXX is a plastic tube resembling those used to sell plastic toy animals.It is clear however a label on the interior blocks view of the inside. The label displays an unknown carnivorous dinosaur and several sauropods, with bright letters that read: “Dr. Wondertainment’s Terrible Lizards!”. Opposite this text is another block of text that says: “Have you ever wanted your very own pet dinosaur? Well now with the newest invention from the minds of Dr. Wondertainment, you can! Simply shake the patented Time Tube and you can have your own tiny tyrant!”

When opened, the tube appears to have a mirror covering the opening, however when shaken onto a surface, a SCP-XXXX-1 instance will be manifested.

SCP-XXXX-1 instances are small animate toys, made of plastic and resembling various prehistoric creatures, most commonly reptiles.
