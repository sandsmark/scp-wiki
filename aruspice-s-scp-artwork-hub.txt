> Welcome to my artwork page, hope you enjoy it! I'm Aruspice, 
> currently working as one of the main artists of SCP: Unity (https://www.scp-unity.com/),
> an SCP game project! 
> 
> In my free time I like making SCP artwork as it's both great practice 
> and more SCPs could use a little visual interpretation. 
> I believe doing that helps them become more known and even iconic in the community. 
> 
> Feel free to comment on the discussion page or DM me and suggest some SCPs you'd like to see 
> represented and I'll try to get to the ones I feel really interested in.
