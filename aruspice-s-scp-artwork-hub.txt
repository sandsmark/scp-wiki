title:Aruspice's SCP artwork hub
Welcome to my artwork page, hope you enjoy it! I'm Aruspice, currently working as one of the main artists of SCP: Unity (https://www.scp-unity.com/), an SCP game project! In my free time I like making SCP artwork as it's both great practice and I feel like more SCPs could use a little visual interpretation as it helps them become more known and even iconic in the community. Feel free to DM me and suggest some SCPs you'd like to see represented and I'll try to get to the ones I feel really interested in.
