__**Dean Has Arrived**__

THE DOMINIONS AND FACULTY OF ALEXYLVA UNIVERSITY HAVE BEEN BESTOWED A PRODIGAL DEAN, WHO SHALL PRESIDE OVER DRAMATICS DESIGNED FOR THE USHERANCE OF KNOWLEDGE AND GREATER INNOVATION. TRULY WISE AND RICH.

ALL PATRONS OF KNOWLEDGE WILL BE ATTENDING. THOSE WHO ARE UNABLE TO ATTEND WILL BE GIVEN A STONE RECORD OF WHAT MASTER THEY SHALL NOW BE ASSIGNED TO.
