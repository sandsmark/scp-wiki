__**A DEAN DAWN ARRIVES**__

THE DEAN OF THE UNIVERSITY HAS RETURNED WITH GREATER STABILITY THAN THE UNIVERSITY HAS KNOWN SINCE THE ASSUMPTION OF LEADERSHIP. FUNDING FOR MULTIPLE ALEXLYVA STUDENT PROGRAMS. CELEBRATION CEREMONIES HAVE BEEN SCHEDULED.

ALL PATRONS OF KNOWLEDGE WILL BE ATTENDING. DO NOT EXPECT TO LEAVE BEFORE SUNDOWN. NO MORE THAN ONE SPEECH WILL BE ENGAGED. A PARTNERSHIP WITH ONEIROI IS EXPECTED TO BE ANNOUNCED.

WORKERS MUST PREPARE TO EXHIBIT RESPECT AND TAKE NOTES ON ANY SPEAKING EMANATING FROM THE DEAN. LOYALTY PLEDGES WILL BE BRANDED IF A LACK OF ENTHUSIASM IS DETECTED.

DO NOT MAKE EYE CONTACT WITH THE DEAN.

__**STUDENTS IN CONFUSION**__

IF UNCERTAINTY HAS STRUCK YOU, ADRIFT IN LIFE, PERHAPS CURSED OR BEWITCHED, ALEXYLVA HAS ACADEMIC MASTERS READY TO ASSIST YOU THROUGHOUT YOUR LIFE. SUBMITTING TO THEIR MASTERY WILL ALLOW YOU TO ABSORB THEIR SKILLS THROUGH ACTION. ADVISORY DRONES ARE AVAILABLE TO DIRECT YOU IN THE PRECISE COURSE THE CATALOG DEMANDS. THERE ARE SEVERAL NEW REQUIREMENTS.

* HUSCII - INSTRUCTION ON SPEAKING WITH OTHER LIVING CREATURES AND SEVERAL NON-LIVING BEINGS. KEEN WILL REQUIRED FROM RESOURCE ALCOVE.
* TRSLXXX - SKILLS AT UTILIZING COMPLEX ABACUS DEVICES AND QUANTIFIERS. LEARN WHAT HAPPENS IN THE EVENT OF NULL TRANSACTION VOIDS.
* FLYDCI - INTRODUCTION TO PHYSICAL ENGAGEMENT, LEARN HOW TO OPERATE BASIC SELF-DEFENSE TOOLS AND BUILD UP PHYSICAL STRENGTH WITH ENDURANCE. CODE MAGAZINE IS REQUIRED
* CRTCCCL - LEARNING THE BEST METHODS OF TRADE AND HOW TO USE TEARS IN THE WORLD FOR FINDING PRODUCTS. THIS WILL ALSO ALLOW FOR ESTABLISHED GUILD LEADERS TO SELECT NEW APPRENTICES.
* MAKDCL - SCULPTING AND CHISELING FOR THE BEGINNER.
[[=]]
SOME MATERIALS ARE REQUIRED FOR ALL CLASSES:
[[/=]]
* STONE CHISEL.
* TABLET.
* PROPER ROBE AND FLOWING GOWN.
* NUTRITION ENOUGH TO ALLOW CORRECT THINKING.
* LIGHTED INSECT IN SEALED VESSEL.
[[=]]
MEMBERS OF ALEXYLVA DIRECTORY CAN BE REACHED THROUGH BIRDING FEATHERMESSAGE OR ELECTRONIC MAIL.
