**“Alias” Personnel File**

**Security Clearance Level:** 3

**Occupation:** Field Agent (non-combat)

**Description:**  “Alias” claims to have no given name and was initially reluctant to take an alias, claiming that 'names are dangerous' (background research has tentatively identified him as Mr. ████ ██████, who [REDACTED].)  Following an unfortunate encounter with SCP-██, Alias has taken to wearing gloves and a full face-mask, leaving no skin exposed.  Alias tests █ standard deviations above normal in agility and has demonstrated an exceptional survival instinct despite his lack of formal combat or weapons training (some such training has been supplied by the Foundation since his induction.)  As such, he has been assigned to join potentially dangerous expeditions as an 'observer' and report back to the Foundation in the event of failure or significant loss of personnel on such an expedition.  Other agents have begun to consider his presence on a mission bad luck.

**History:**  Alias joined the Foundation after becoming involved in the original retrieval of [[[SCP-247]]], in which the retrieval team credit him with preventing unacceptable casualties by informing the team of SCP-247's harmful properties before they entered its range of effect.  Prior to [DATA EXPUNGED BY REQUEST].

Alias has a Bachelor's Degree in Dentistry from the University of Toronto.
