**“Alias” Personnel File**

**Security Clearance Level:** 3

**Occupation:** Field Agent

**Description:**  Agent "Alias" (formerly Agent ████ ████████, who was given a full amnesiac treatment after a Keter-class incident involving [REDACTED]) is a first-response agent, assigned to investigate unverified reports of paranormal activity.  [EXPUNGED] Alias has taken to wearing gloves and a full face-mask, leaving no skin exposed.  Alias has an unusual reaction to darkness, claiming to find it comforting rather than frightening, and has expressed a preference for assignments that take him to isolated underground locations.

Alias suffers from severe myopia and has been equipped with specially designed prescription night-vision goggles for use on missions.
