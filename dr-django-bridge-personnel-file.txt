**Name:** Dr. Django Bridge
**Security Clearance:** 2
**Duties:** Assistant Researcher. 

**History:** Dr. Bridge [EXPUNGED]. He was then transferred to Site ██ and given training. After proving himself capable of remembering perfectly the designation of all SCP objects he has clearance for, Bridge [REDACTED]. 

Dr. Bridge enjoys tinkering, and frequently attempts to create elaborate variations of everyday appliances, vehicles, equipment, machines, tools, and so on. After initial orientation, Dr. Bridge requisitioned $████ worth of equipment to build a rollerblade-powered delivery system. [DATA REDACTED] following the invention of a gasoline powered combat armour covered in chainsaw-like blades, Bridge attempted to produce a new, automatically encrypted OS for Foundation computer systems.  However, in final testing stages, [EXPUNGED]. Dr. Bridge is no longer allowed to requisition funds for his inventions.  

Furthermore, he may not be promoted to a higher position until 2027. Dr. Bridge is under psychiatric probation until December 2012.
