**Item #:** ğ’«

**Object Class:** Keter

**Disambiguation:** ğ’« may refer to either part of the interconnected anomaly described below. 
* ğ’«, when described in physical situations or as performing actions, refers to the individual. 
* ğ’«, when described with regards to memetic effects or written/spoken transmission, refers to the word.

**Special Containment Procedures:** Due in part to ğ’«'s docility and compliance with the Foundation, ğ’«'s containment has been adapted to allow for as much autonomy as possible, including janitorial duties and scheduling interviews/therapy.

These orifices are largely symmetrical, with minor discrepancies across the midline of the face, and are in a constant state of flux, having new ones generated while the cranial flesh simultaneously absorbs existing ones. 

ğ’« also refers to the word "ğ’«", which displays a number of memetic qualities with regards to its transmission, both auditory and visual. ğ’«, the individual, cannot be referred to by any name or direct identifier other than "ğ’«". When written or otherwise transferred visually, all direct reference to ğ’« is limited to the word "ğ’«". However, when spoken, the word is pronounced identically to a separate word from the English language. A spoken rendition of the word "ğ’«" may be heard below.
