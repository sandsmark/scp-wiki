[[tab About]]

I am by no means a veteran of this site. I lurked around for several years, and I joined in late 2017, only getting super invested around the time of the 3000 contest. Since then, I've visited the site daily. I tried making an article once, but it failed. And most of my other ideas are boring. So what did I do? I made pixel art. 

I started making pixel art a //loooong// time ago, making texture packs for the game //Minecraft.// My style has since developed from solid color blobs to a //Binding of Isaac//-esque style. This, combined with the SCP community, made the perfect place for me to make and post pixel art. And then it expanded a bit, see below.
