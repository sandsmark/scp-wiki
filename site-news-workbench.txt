[[size 75%]]This is a series showing off news from the site, articles from the past month[[footnote]] with a rating above 0 [[/footnote]], and fan-content for you to check out and discover! Be sure to leave a comment with your thoughts![[/size]]

++++* Fandom Stuff

* Many of the SCP wikis celebrated April 1st. On the English wiki many SCP pages changed their colors and turned into [http://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/april-fools-2020 Super Cool Plants].

* On the ZH wiki, The Darkroom 2020 Event of the Mobile Task Forces has concluded. 11 authors submitted 8 SCPs and 4 tales featuring MTFs which are eligible for secret prizes.

* The Spanish-speaking wiki finally made it to 200 unique SCP articles and thus held the SCP-ES-200 contest. 9 authors submitted 7 articles, but only one took home the prize of being labeled ES-200.

* The Czech branch's [http://scp-cs.wikidot.com/cz-contest-iv-hub Fourth Contest] ended on April 30th. Contestants wrote for the theme "Czech Foundation", with submitted articles showing a bit of the history of the Czech branch.

* The [http://wanderers-library.wikidot.com/wanderers-depths-contest Wanderers Depths Contest] on the Wanderers' Library wiki began this month. A writing contest based on works that take place on or under the Seven Seas (or anything related to the Maritime).

* The Russian branch SCP-RU has announced its 14th contest - [http://scpfoundation.net/loneliness-and-oblivion-contest The Isolation Contest] in which contestants have to either work with the theme of quarantine or large-scale loss of memories in their articles.

+* Q&A:

++* Q: Can I replace a blurb that you wrote for one of my articles with my own? I'm not satisfied with it.

A: Absolutely. You will always have that option at any point.

++* Q: I noticed an error/typo/incorrect information and wish to correct it. May I?

A: Sure! If the changes are not limited to your own article(s) and/or are substantial changes across multiple entries, you should post in that month's discussion page and then make the changes.

++* Q: I wrote an article and it seems to have stuck. Can I send a blurb of mine to one of the frequent collaborators?

A: Yup! It's much easier if you come to us about it, and it gives you the control over what you want shown on the news page.
