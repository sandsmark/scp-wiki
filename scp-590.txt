**Item #:** SCP-590

**Object Class:** Safe

**Special Containment Procedures:** 590 is assigned to a regular SCP living space at Sector 7. There is to be one guard on duty at all times within said space, to ensure 590 does not harm himself. 590 is to be supplied with one television, which is to be used only in the broadcasting of children's shows. At no time is 590 allowed to leave Sector 7, for any reason. Subject is to be fed only the vitamin laced protein gruel created for 590. Anyone caught attempting to feed 590 anything else will receive Keter duty. SCP-590 is not to be handled without proper authorisation.

**Note:**590 is not to be named as anything other then Five Ninety. He is a tool to be used, not someones friend, sibling, or child. Any one found forming attachments to an SCP will be removed to a less people  intensive duty.

**Description** SCP-590 appears to be a young male, aproximately 16 years of age.The only benefit he recieves from his powers is apparent longevity, as he has not aged since his arrival at the Foundation.

Although in all aspects a normal teenager,  when 590
touches any other human, he heal all injuries and ailments, physical and mental, they may have.
