[[span style="font-size: 200%; font-family: 'Pirata One', serif;"]] [http://www.scp-wiki.net/scp-5770 SCP-5770 - The Festival of Florence] [[/span]]
[[span style="font-size: 120%; font-family: 'IM Fell English', serif;"]]
[[collapsible show="▶Author Commentary◀" hide="▼Author Commentary▼"]]
[[<]]
This was my first article, but not my first idea. I fell into the trap of 'the-thing-that-does-a-thing' and started writing an article about a Sharpie that when you write an adjective on something it becomes that. For example, I write indestructible on myself with the marker and I become indestructible. Not a very good idea, eh?

Back to 5770, my first idea was that it would just be this festival where people died inspired by the punishments of //Inferno// by Dante. Not very interesting. It wasn't until I spoke with a few members of the Butterfly Squad for the article to become a pseudo-metaphor for the Foundation. The //Inferno// references remained and it still my favorite part of the article.

It was an extremely long article once upon a time, almost 12,000 words at one point. What happened was I wrote an Aud/Vis log for //every day// that the festival would have taken place. I was told multiple times that it was too long and I cut it down immensely. I also put //waaaay// too much symbolism/references into this thing:
* The Festival started the same year Dante died.
* The -1 and -2 entities' names are derived from the meaning of Virgil and Dante, the main characters of //Inferno.//
* Most, if not, all of the last names of the people killed during 5770 have some meaning/connection to the Christian/Abrahamic God.
* MTF Zeta-66 is 666, 'Guardian Angels' is self-explanatory.
* Frozen-Over is also self-explanatory.
* Battalion-36 "Colossians" and Battalion-21 "Romans" are direct references to bible verses that speak about sin and hypocrisy. //Foreshadowing...//
* Pasqua is 'Easter' in Italian, Easter is when Jesus was reborn in the Christian faith, referencing Pasqua's rebirth. It's also a reference to my last name which is derived from Pasqua.
* The whole article is a reference to my Italian heritage but no one would have gotten that without knowing me in real life.
* The number 7 is sprinkled throughout the article.

And I've probably missed some.

It's not the best article and it has many flaws. I will eventually go back and rewrite a lot of it someday, but, the core narrative is good though and I'm proud of it, but definite work is needed.

[[/<]]

[[span style="font-size: 200%; font-family: 'Pirata One', serif;"]][http://www.scp-wiki.net/scp-5322 SCP-5322 - Dying of the Light][[/span]]
[[span style="font-size: 120%; font-family: 'IM Fell English', serif;"]]
[[collapsible show="▶Author Commentary◀" hide="▼Author Commentary▼"]]
[[<]]
This was an interesting article to write. I kinda just came up with the idea and started writing. My main 'message' was for how we as people give things power through our perception. There's also a splash of Greek Mythology; the entity is a mixture of depictions of Helios and Apollo and their traits.

I took some inspiration in the event log of [http://www.scp-wiki.net/scp-5001 SCP-5001]. Mostly in the Foundation's protocol and such.

5322 is a somewhat self-contained article which was my intention. Greek mythology was one of my favorite subjects when I was younger so this will not be my first reference to it in my articles. You can see a definite improvement between 5770 and 5322, probably because [[*user MalyceGraves]] ripped 5322 in half and sewed it back together.

[[span style="font-size: 200%; font-family: 'Pirata One', serif;"]][http://www.scp-wiki.net/scp-5594 SCP-5594 - Snail Racing at the End of Reality][[/span]]
[[span style="font-size: 120%; font-family: 'IM Fell English', serif;"]]
[[collapsible show="▶Author Commentary◀" hide="▼Author Commentary▼"]]
[[<]]
This is a rather goofy article. The story behind this one is, I went to a random word generator website and out popped out 'snail,' 'pyramid,' 'sacrifice,' 'morale,' and 'beginning.' I then started looking up how the hell I could connect snails to pyramids and that's where I learned about snail racing. I said 'huh, how about a reality-bending snail race?' and I wrote this.

I tried to take this article seriously but I crumbled and added in an **excessive** amount of puns, and I think they make the article about 20 times better.

I don't have too much to say about this one other than that I loved reading and writing this one.
[[/<]]

[[span style="font-size: 120%; font-family: 'IM Fell English', serif;"]]
[[collapsible show="▶Author Commentary◀" hide="▼Author Commentary▼"]]

This is another extremely goofy article. A bit of backstory, my friend and I were discussing if other animals can be trained similarly to dogs. He mentioned how funny it would be if you could give a crab a dollar and it'll bring you a beer. Of course, I adjusted this to make it a little more emotional, plus the whole cold war thing was a fun way to take this one.
