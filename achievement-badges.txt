[[>]]
[[module Rate]]

Allow me to borrow these paragraphs from [http://www.scp-wiki.net/scp-achievement-badges this page], it is also what I'm about to say:
[[collapsible show="+ So here it is" hide="- thanks"]]
> This is a just-for-fun type of page for people to list their accomplishments and provide badges for display on their author pages. Please leave a comment listing every achievement you are eligible for along with any relevant links to verify your claim. This is optional for now, we're going by the honor system. After all, anyone caught clout-chasing by claiming achievements they hadn't earned would inevitably be discovered and reveal the weak character of the perpetrator.
> 
> Keep entries at approximate Tweet-length, if they're too long they will be trimmed. Try not to have excessively long lists of links. Keep it clean and classy.
> 
> Feel free to leave suggestions in the discussion page for future achievements to be added in the comments. This page is intended to grow over time so check back occasionally for new achievements.
[[/collapsible]]

Yet here is my own word: 
[[collapsible show="+ Thanks" hide="- thanks"]]
I did not mean to measure anything by the ratings, or deny the fact that we are writing as a hobby, in this page. After all this is a "for fun" page, it may be quite informal with various visible problems in it. I'm sorry if any content in this page make you feel uncomfortable, but trust me I didn't mean it. Thank you for your reading. 
[[/collapsible]]
