Welcome to the Rounderpage, affectionately referred to as Hell! If you look above to the ACS, you'll see some of the formerly-magnificent pistons that were been described by former patrons as "oddly sexual", and "fucking weird". If you scroll down, you'll see a small text exchange which serves to introduce you, dear reader, to my body of work! You scroll a little further down and you'll see a nice little section reserved for reviews of the Rounderpage. Enjoy your stay!
