title:Gabriel Jade's Personnel File
Despite repeated requests to submit, and failed attempts by the Foundation to gather,[[footnote]]//Seriously it's been the better part of a decade, just submit the damned forms!// - Site Director [REDACTED][[/footnote]] detailed personal information regarding Dr. Gabriel Jade has not been made available to Human Resources. 

The following list of anomalous research has been credited to Dr. Jade based on the series of hastily written containment theories haphazardly pieced together which provided initial containment procedures for the following anomalies:

> [[[SCP-049|SCP-049: Plague Doctor]]] - All data pertaining to 049 was eventually and painstakingly pieced together by [[[http://www.scp-wiki.net/djkaktus|a team of intrepid detectives and their pet dog]]], who subsequently [[[[http://www.scp-wiki.net/scp-049-arc|REDACTED]]]] happily on a farm.
> 
> [[[SCP-077|SCP-077: Rot Skull]]] - Translated from original Hebrew present on post-it notes to modern English by [[[http://www.scp-wiki.net/researcher-voct-s-personnel-file|Researcher Voct.]]]
> 
> [[[SCP-3394|SCP-3394: Brain Scientist]]] - Digital compilation ran to completion, and provided a workable document[[footnote]]Revised by multiple members of Site-19 staff.[[/footnote]]
> 
> [[[http://www.scp-wiki.net/4000contestgabrieljade|SCP-4███: A Critique of Man]]] - Original containment procedures were recovered from markings on a stained and crumpled napkin within O5-7's desk drawer.
