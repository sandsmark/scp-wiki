**Discovery Log:** 10/13/2014

In the early morning of October 13th, 2014, an anomalous broadcast was detected originating somewhere in southern Indiana. Further investigation led Mobile Task Force Kappa-8 "Weed Whackers" to an apartment building in [DATA EXPUNGED], where it was discovered that the source of the broadcast was an animate, talking cactus. Foundation personnel have allowed for the flowering desert plant, which refers to itself as D.J. Kaktus, to continue to live at its original point of discovery[[footnote]]I know this kind of breaks protocol, but it's really spiky and it pokes us if we get too close and nobody wants to move it.[[/footnote]]. 

To escape the tedium of this confinement, the cactus has begun to transmit a series of weekly[[footnote]]lol weekly if I //feel like it motherfuckers//[[/footnote]] podcasts. The identities of the show's guests are unknown[[footnote]]I mean, look at these weird fucking names. How are we supposed to find these people?[[/footnote]].
