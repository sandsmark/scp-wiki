+ Person of Interest File #02222/01 — "Aethris"

**Legal Name:** ███████ ████

**Known Aliases:** Aethris, Aethris_Art, Aestrei

**Reason for Interest:** Person of Interest possesses a sizable collection of memetic artwork, despite having no connections to any known anart-related Groups of Interest. Potentially a Type-III ontokinetic entity.

**Description:** Aethris (appearing as Aestrei and Aethris_Art on the Reddit and Twitter social media platforms respectively) is an amateur producer of anomalous art with a wide variety of different styles, ranging from digital painting to pixel art.
