[[tab Big Friend]]

**Friend Name:** Randall Bannock

**Birthday:** Uhhh too big a number

**Best Friend Number:** Three

**Job:** White coat man

**Friend Details:** Bread man is a really good friend! He has long red hair and smells like cake! He gives me munchies that he makes when I do real good and they are very yummy crispy crunchy!  He also got me a munchy bed after I did a real good job munching a red man and telling him about a bad orange pat person.  I see him scribble on his scribble board sometimes too. I hope I get to be as good a scribbler one time!
