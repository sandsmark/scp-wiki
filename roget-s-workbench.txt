+ Artist Showcase

[[include component:image-block name=http://68.media.tumblr.com/bc3ead9738163d6dc734373dfc246efa/tumblr_nyp3xcFYuh1uc7jxqo5_1280.png|caption=example from Broken Masquerade legal firm advertisements [click image to enlarge]|link="http://scp-wiki.wdfiles.com/local--files/scp-2901/SCP-2901-edit1.jpg"]]

[[div style="background-color: #2e4050; background: linear-gradient(to right, #666,#fff,#fff); padding: 0px 8px;"]]
++ [[span style="text-shadow: #003 2px 2px; color: #fff;"]]Cyantreuse[[/span]]
[[/div]]

"This image is one of many from my series of Broken Masquerade legal firm ads. The way I see it, with the Foundation being public in [[[broken-masquerade-hub|Broken Masquerade's setting,]]] organizations dedicated to forming legally stable grounds for arguments against the Foundation's morals and methods and/or helping human and sapient anomalies get back on their feet (if/when released from containment) are going to have a presence.

For Leeward & Warner Legal Solutions, they've got a sensitive backstory that I haven't bothered to write yet, but Leeward is Adam Leeward of the [[[SCP-2708]]] mini-canon, and Warner is... well, maybe you can figure it out from the [[[too-old-to-lie-too-cold-to-love|preexisting context,]]] but I haven't bothered to elaborate on that in writing yet, so you'll just have to wait.

I use Adobe Photoshop 2017 through Creative Cloud, because I'm one of those suckers who likes accessibility and rip-off subscriptions. For this specific work, my own photography was used, though I sometimes use images 'licensed for reuse' through Google Image Search if I need to combine a bunch of different images."
