[[collapsible show="▼ Researcher Felicity show" hide="△ Researcher Felicity hide" hideLocation="both"]]
This is Felicity Torres, a researcher at the SCP Foundation Research and Development division that I drew/made up using a set of suggested words [http://www.scp-wiki.net/forum/t-779913/practice-drawing-researchers#post-1974186 from users]. Any are welcome to use her for role playing or writing!

^^Adjectives and other requested words for this one suggested by OZ Ouroboros:^^

^^* Female^^
^^* Petite^^
^^* Apathetic^^
^^* Spontaneous^^
^^* Research & Development^^
^^* Dragon Fruit^^

^^She's not terribly tall, though not terribly short either, though she's rather thin and most of her clothing is baggy on her. She can be rather emotionless, though behind her dead stare her mind pours over data at a rapid rate. Mentally jumping from topic to topic, she can very quickly come to unique conclusions and prefers action over having to take the time to explain herself to those around her. Often these actions involve impromptu tests for some newly formed hypothesis she just came up with.^^

^^She doesn't like to talk beyond what is necessary and often gets lost in her own train of thought. Since she usually shows very little, if any, external warnings, her impulsive actions often surprise those around her. Felicity is very much an iceberg, with a lot more going on in her head that she shows. In a sense, she is a reversed Dragon Fruit. Her outside is bland, while inside her head is an explosive storm of thoughts.^^

[[image https://pre00.deviantart.net/adeb/th/pre/f/2014/054/8/7/researcher_felicity_scp_foundation_by_toadking07-d77s9zd.png]]
[[/collapsible]]
