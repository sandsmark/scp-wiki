[[tabview]]
[[tab Donkman: The Overview]]
**Who is Ulysses B. Donkman:**
Donkman, or Donk for short, is an entity capable of warping both perception or reality (depending on your interpretation*). He's wacky at times, serious at others, but most importantly always finding himself caught in an adventure. He's the old fashion American folk hero with a new spin.
[[collapsible show="Creator's Note*" hide="DrAkimoto's Ramblings about Donkman"]]
I see Donkman as a passive reality bender that thinks he is capable of changing perception. That's where his real limitations are, he's only as strong and fast as needs to be, which at times is tremendous but much less than what he is capable of. It's also why he shows up on film, he thinks he just changes the perception of himself, so in reality, that's what it is. I'm rambling that's enough.
[[/collapsible]]

**Where is Ulysses B. Donkman:**
//Well, the real Donkman has been inside us all along.//

But really, anywhere in Southern USA and Mexico fits his theme well. We don't want Donkman to be restrictive at all, so if you can think of a good reason why he's somewhere else–that's fine too. 

**When is Ulysses B. Donkman:**
I'd place the beginning of Donkman's journey at somewhere in the early 1800s lasting up until the present, or even //the future//. Donkman does not age so there are really no restraints in that regard. 

**Why is Ulysses B. Donkman:**
Ah yes, the biggest questions in life always start with //why?//.
Put simply: because he can. Donkman is a man without a plan, until he has one. He's out for adventure, making friends, sharing drinks, saving the day, and always making sure he comes out on top. He's Chaotic Good in its Donkest form.
[[/tab]]
[[tab How to Write]]
WHO ARE WE TO TELL YOU HOW TO WRITE
@@ @@
From [[*user DrAkimoto]]: Really, all I can say is have fun with it. Read the article and tales get a feel for the Donk adventures and take it from there. The key is a mixture of serious and wacky, a story with action, wit, and folksy adventure. Try staying down to earth when it comes to the situation and then let your imagination run wild on the execution.
