+* Who I Am
[[/div]]

Hello.

Most people refer to me as [[*user Nagiros]], although I also accept Nagi for short. I use she/her and they/them pronouns, and I'm currently 18. I'm a believer in music and emotion. I've got a lot to learn about this site and life in general.

I review articles posted to the Idea and Draft forums, as well as creating my own SCPs, tales, and GOI Formats ‚Äî you may see me around sometimes, and I'm always happy to help if you want some eyes on a draft.

I'm most well-known for making a lot of contributions to the [[[Site-17 Hub | Site-17 Deepwell Catalog]]] and creating/maintaining the [[[Esoteric Classes Complete List | Comprehensive List of Esoteric Classes]]]. I have aspirations of doing more ‚Äî I want to be prolific and have a formidable backlog of content I can be proud of. I want to inspire others.

We'll see.

+ SCP-4051

Well, here we are. First SCP. The one that started the Deepwell Catalog, even if that wasn't my intent when I posted it. I'm still surprised people liked it.

4051 is‚Ä¶ complex. It's a story about a teenage vigilante superhero who joins a task force and unlocks a hidden power by the end ‚Äî and it's all played straight. I had no aspiration of commenting on well-known tropes which have been known to kill articles on this site. I just wanted to write a story despite them.

4051 was a cry for help. The Nagiros who posted it was trapped in a hostile setting, tormented by authority figures who should have been looking after her, stuck in a mindspace of passive acceptance and denial.

I've had a lot of requests to continue Rainer's story. As I've grown as a person, he's remained at the sidelines: foraying with [[[SCP-4260 | Death]]], finding some [[[SCP-4415 | friends]]], inspiring [[[Systems Patch | revolution]]]. I'd like to continue that story.

+ SCP-4175

I wrote this for Pride Month of 2019 and it's currently my most popular article. Personally? I don't think it's my best work.

Then again, a lot of people liked it. I know a lot of readers were excited to see the trans pride flag on a featured article ‚Äî even if it did bring a fair amount of controversy my way.

+ AFTERLIFE OPHIDIAN /// The Foundation Over Heaven

A poetry-prose piece based in despair. I felt trapped in life, faced by oblivion on all sides, and channeled it into this article.

It exists, I suppose.

+ SCP-4755

Now then, it looks like my pet anomalous science, essophysics, has finally come into its own. The Foundation's tasted the blood in the water and they aren't backing down. If the universe rejects them, then the solution is simple: build a better universe.

Expect great things in the future.
