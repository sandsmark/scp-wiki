Welcome to this installment of site news, where something, something May the Fourth meme!

This is a series showing off news from the site, articles from the past month[[footnote]] with a rating above 0 [[/footnote]], and fan-content for you to check out and discover! Be sure to leave a comment with your thoughts!

++++ Featured Articles

* The featured articles for May were [[[SCP-2881]]] "The Tree You Cannot Climb" by [[*user Zmax15]], [[[Astronomical]]] by [[*user Sophia Light]], and P'rantortiz the Vile by [[*user Shaggydredlocks]]! The first is about an escalating threat is put to the test, the second a chilling account of a dying world, and the third is a Black Queen-article about an ancient evil! Check 'em out!
