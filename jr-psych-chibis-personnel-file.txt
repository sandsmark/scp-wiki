title:Junior Psychologist "Chibi" Yamagusuku's Personnel File
Name: Blake Maxson Yamagusuku, goes by nickname of "Chibi" or "Chibs"

Security Clearance: Level 2

Position: Junior Psychologist

Current Location: Site 17

Current Assignment: Junior Psychologist Yamagusuku is currently at Site 17 aiding research with psychological testing of Safe humanoid SCPs, and with personnel psychological evaluations as needed.

[[collapsible show="+ SPECIAL CLEARANCE REQUIRED, PLEASE PROVIDE IDENTIFICATION" hide="- ETHICS COMMITTEE IDENTIFICATION ACCEPTED, LOADING . . ."]]
Junior Psychologist Yamagusuku is currently involved in the Ethics Committee's Internal Monitoring division, acting as an Observer for Site 17. Their current assignment is observation of mental and emotional well-being of contained humanoid SCPs at Site 17, but they are expected to report any ethics breaches outside of that assignment as well. As with all Observers, Observer Yamagusuku is given Level 5 clearance while acting within the role of Observer. This clearance is restricted to use for only Observer or other Ethics Committee-approved purposes.
[[/collapsible]]
