Missing out on articles? Enjoy a recap on the articles from the past month, as October has come and gone, and managed to bring us some spooky things![[footnote]][[size 80%]]Summaries may not be strictly informative. Your mileage may vary.[[/size]][[/footnote]]

+++ Week of October 1st

Not much happened this week. However, we did get some cool stuff. You guys didn't happen to wander into a Halloween store, did you?

* [[[SCP-2523]]] ("Goblin Market", a rewrite of "Halloween Store") - From Halloween to November 1st, these stores are full of folklore, merriment, and decoration! Buy spooky stuff, so long as you barter something of yours for it. Go buy your orchard fruits, go buy, go buy! Originally written by [[*user Foamfollower]], whimsically rewritten by [[*user sirpudding]].
* [[[SCP-2985]]] ("Anart, Author of the Scream") - These paintings are perfect replications... perhaps too perfect. An artist struggles to get his message heard while bigger players lurk in the shadows. Written by [[*user TyGently]].

* [[[sunny-art-scp-tarot-cards|SunnyClockwork's Artwork - SCP Tarot Cards]]] - Ever wanted SCP-themed tarot cards for you and your friends? Look no further than Sunny's art! It's basically what you'd want out of an SCP-themed occult setting, minus the actual ghosts. Created by [[*user SunnyClockwork]].

+++ Week of October 8th

Loads of cool content. These articles are my BFFs.

* [[[SCP-2286]]] ("If You Build a Better Mousetrap…") - If you build an attractor for a mice, you may try it out on people. And if you build an attractor for people... Written by [[*user thehermit2]].
* [[[SCP-2790]]] ("You've Got a Squid in Me") - Aww, how adorable! We should play and cuddle them! They're so cute, aren't they? Gorgeous skin too. Cutely written by [[*user A Random Day]].
* [[[SCP-7394-J]]] ("HILARIOUS FARTING FROG CLICK NOW TO SEE") - Dude, did you get a load of this frog during lunch break? It let rip this giant, awful fart. We couldn't stop cracking up! #keterfrogfarts courtesy of [[*user Salman Corbette]] and [[*user Faminepulse]].
* [[[SCP-2298]]] ("Life in a Plastic Box") - Go into a shed, and you'll come across a city of mannequins. A closer look shows it's not just a world of fake plastic dummies and fake plastic trees. Written by [[*user Eekium]].
* [[[SCP-2580]]] ("Father-Daughter Project") - Father and daughter build a rocket to send to some aliens far off in the galaxy.. Written by [[*user Zmax15]].
* [[[SCP-2528]]] ("Panda Processors") - Computers that see the world change over and over, and no one will listen. All they can do is be trapped and scream. Written by [[*user thamuzz]].

* [[[document-48116-88-son-of-the-earth|Document 48116-88 ("Son of the Earth")]]] - My dearest Mashen'ka, I hope you and him are happy together, for these lost words are all I am able to offer again. Written by [[*user Blackbird5154]] and [[*user Iavev]], translated by [[*user Gene R]].
* [[[a-witch-s-tale-mistakes-were-made|A Witch's Tale - Mistakes Were Made]]] - The cost of art is nothing compared to the message it'll send... or so they'll tell you. Take it from a witch; God hates art. Written by [[*user sirpudding]].

* [[[dreamwalkerfae-s-author-page|DreamwalkerFae's Author Page]]] - [[*user DreamwalkerFae]]'s author page! Greetings o' weary traveler. Come in, and sit down, and let Fae weave you a story about those who lived on.
* [[[CirclesAndSquares Author Page]]] - [[*user CirclesAndSquares]]' author page! Though, uh, someone should look into Dr. Bessette's unusual behavior.

+++ Week of October 15th

If you're feeling sinister, go off and see your minister...

* [[[SCP-2459]]] ("When the Traffic Clears") - These are all my possessions, and I can't just leave them. Would you walk up and away from all you hold dear or believe in? Written by [[*user WrongJohnSilver]].
* [[[SCP-2741]]] ("A Sinister Device") - A HA HA HA HA HA! ARE YOU READY TO FEEL... SINISTER? READ THE ARTICLE WHEN READY, AND THINGS WILL BECOME TRULY… SINISTER!! Written by [[*user Freudian]].
* [[[SCP-2106]]] ("A Conspiracy of Ravens") - The ravens flock together to feel human again. The human feels like a raven. This dynamic is too delicate to disturb. Written by [[*user Jacob Conwell]].
* [[[SCP-2428]]] ("Mr. Mad") - Sanity is relative. And in the wacky world of Wondertainment, Mr. Mad is the odd one out. Written by [[*user TwistedGears]].
* [[[SCP-2910]]] ("Welt am Draht") - Ever feel like people are ignoring you on purpose? They are. Forever. Written by [[*user A Random Day]].

* [[[Home Again]]] - ""As I looked through the lens, the light…the flowers…the soldier returning to his lover after a deployment in hell…all of it suddenly felt perfect." Written by [[*user Snarky Potato]].
* [[[a-swingsets-song|A Swingset's Song]]] - I failed three times, abysmal; alone and guilty, tragic, Knowing that my ledger's full; red from misuse, magic. Written by [[*user Researcher Dios]], but you'll probably forget about it.
* [[[Fear Not]]] - "Do you understand literary allusion and quantum mechanics? Neither does anyone else, that's why this is still only +18." The previous blurb definitely not written by [[*user Randomini]] or anything, but the tale was.

* [[[SCP-723-J]]] ("Sad Roach") - Sad roach is sad. I wonder what happened to that roach to make it so sad. It must have had a hard life. Sadness transcribed by [[*user Zyn]] and [[*user Silberescher]].

* [[[hallowinside|Hallow Inside]]] - A Broken God member and a Sakricst priest trick-or-treat for Halloween, and find time to make some awkward talk. Written by [[*user HatIsMyFriend]].

* [[[freudian|Dr Jung's Terminal]]] - [[*user Freudian]]'s author page! Dr. Jung is away, but leave him an email and he may get back to you in a timely manner.

+++ Week of October 29th

* [[[SCP-2944]]] ("An Anomalous Haircut") - It is, indeed, an anomalous haircut. A pretty ugly one, if you ask me. Is it worth it to control all the hairs on your body? Written by [[*user Cavalry]].
* [[[SCP-512]]] ("Gravity-Nullifying Umbrella") - A rewrite of the [http://scpclassic.wikidot.com/scp-512 old 512]. Poor Mary Poppins, to say the least. Brought back to life by [[*user Roget]] and [[*user Dexanote]].

+++ Fandom Stuff

What's been going on in the community aside from our site? Well...

* [http://scpfoundation.ru/site-7-midnight-radio Site 7 Midnight Radio] - A podcast done by [[*user iavev]] over on the Russian site! If you understand Russian and wanna take a look, feel free. Be sure to inform us of what they say too!
* ZAP has created [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CjG0lJ_p0Xw SCP MOVIE: Episode 1], a well-shot, tensely-filmed, and atmospheric video about [[[SCP-004]]]. It's in Russian, but with English subtitles, so you can enjoy it!
* This Exists, a YouTube channel pointing out and explaining cool pop culture stuff, did a video on the SCP Wiki! The [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=55igYZ0EShc video] is short and succinct, and while it doesn't cover everything, it's a great entry video for those new.
* [https://www.reddit.com/r/Daily_SCP/ Daily_SCP] is a subreddit that showcases a new SCP a day, from the well-known to the obscure and hidden. A good way to find new skips you may not have heard of; I know I've discovered a couple new favorites from it. It's run by user HighlyDestruct7.
* [http://www.scp-wiki.net/forum/t-1414320 SCP Minecraft] yet again rises from the grave! Servers are run by [[*user Doctor Cimmerian]], check it out!
