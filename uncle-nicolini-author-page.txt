[[tab Dr. Everwood]]
__APPEARS IN__
[[module ListPages separate="no" tags="+doctor-everwood" perPage="40" prependLine="||~ Title ||~ Author ||"]]
||%%title_linked%%||%%created_by%%||

__SUMMARY__
Dr. Everwood is a bright and cheerful person, despite her appearance. She has short brown hair, green eyes, and looks tired most of the time. Is missing her right arm, which she lost in a [DATA EXPUNGED] incident. Doesn't like her prosthetic arm but wears it when necessary. Despite her snark and grump, she knows how to have fun and likes doing so. Identifies as female, but looks relatively androgynous and likes it that way. Born and raised in Florida. Speaks Spanish and English.

You can use her as a GoI specialist if you like, as that's what she enjoys.
Works for the UIU as a rookie agent in the Aces and Eights canon.
Lead Dr. Wondertainment researcher in Wonderful World canon.
Administrator in the canon of [[[SCP-4224]]]
