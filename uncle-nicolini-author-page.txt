[[tab scp]]
__scp-3297__
you remember that doodle you made when you were 7 and you were so proud of it you showed it to all of your friends and they said it was pretty cool, but then you look at it again when you're 24 and you realize it's not that (at all, really) good? yeah, thats how i feel about this one. i wrote this because i was dealing with the death of my grandmother and i have no other coping mechanisms.
__scp-3358__
written with my friend janitor worth before they went on their spirit journey. this article isn't really either of our best work, nor am I really too proud of it, but writing it was a very fun time. this was originally going to be like some island of xmen or whatever before i got my hands on it.
__scp-3803__
conceptualized, written, and published in about an hour. technically 30 minutes but my friend wanted to photoshop an image to use in the article, and im grateful they did. my most successful coldpost.
__scp-3923__
based on my experiences in the white collar business world. god, i hate this business. anyway, yeah. compulsion effects and language. i had a lot of fun writing it because its so silly. also, everything in there are things i heard. all of it. none of it made up.
__scp-4003__
this was hell to write, but im glad i did. i spent so long researching and proofreading and writing and i was so scared to submit it until [[*user djkaktus]] essentially bullied me into submitting it. it was written with a dear irl friend and a cool online friend, both of whom aren't very active nowadays. sad times.
__scp-4299__
this article spent the longest in the oven when compared to anything else. it was rewritten 6 times. 6 times, jesus. my friend cole and i worked hard on it as a form of wish fulfillment to cure our sad boy status.
__scp-4949__
oh man. this one was wild. i approached gabe and told him 'what if dr wondertainment met dado' and then we wrote. we also wrote this FAST so we could snatch the double 49 spot because gabe really likes that number.
__scp-3879__
this one spawned my obsession with having GoI interactions, this time between WWS and Wondertainment. it also got me closer to my dear friend darkstuff, and im so glad i wrote it even though its reception wasnt the best. 
__scp-2983__
written with darkstuff. my first collab with my good bud, written because i think we need more cereal based scp.
__scp-3756__
this was written entirely around the title. just some absurdity. yeehaw, partner.
__scp-4988__
this was written about my hatred of working in a call center for a while and also the exploitation of minorities in other countries for profiteering. or whatever. plague girl got ahold of it during draftswap.
__scp-3863__
COW BEES was originally written as a rewrite of SCP-1912, which got rejected for being different. It all works out because Rimple (who is an excellent author) wanted to rewrite it, and they got it! At time of writing, it hasn't come out yet but I look forward to it. The idea came about because i found out about canulated cows. don't google that. its gross. im serious.
__scp-1712__
Roget is an an author i've always admired, and we're both Florida boys. When this article dipped into the negatives, I took a stab at rewriting it. i took the concept of 'weird shit just happens sometimes' and turned it into an scp. nothing makes sense because it doesn't have to.
__scp-3867__
so one night kirby and i were up at 1 AM talking and I jokingly brought up 'panda watch' and gave updates on whatever trivial bullshit my dog was doing. he said 'what if there was an scp that was a website that told you the last thing your pet did' and then we stayed up till like 2AM brainstorming. i woke up the next day with a draft and he signed off on it.

[[tab Dr. Everwood]]
__APPEARS IN__
[[module ListPages separate="no" tags="+doctor-everwood" perPage="40" prependLine="||~ Title ||~ Author ||"]]
||%%title_linked%%||%%created_by%%||
[[/module]]
__SUMMARY__
Dr. Everwood is a snarky, grumpy person. She has short brown hair, green eyes, and looks tired most of the time. Is missing her right arm, which she lost in a [DATA EXPUNGED] incident. Doesn't like her prosthetic arm but wears it when necessary. Despite her snark and grump, she knows how to have fun and likes doing so. Identifies as female, but looks relatively androgynous and likes it that way. Born and raised in Florida. Speaks Spanish and English.

You can use her as a GoI specialist if you like, as that's what she enjoys.
Works for the UIU as a rookie agent in the Aces and Eights canon.
Lead Dr. Wondertainment researcher in Wonderful World canon.
Administrator in the canon of [[[SCP-4224]]]

__HOW DOES SHE LIKE HER COFFEE?__
Prefers bubble tea, but if forced to drink coffee likes espresso with a little bit of sugar.
__HOW OFTEN DOES SHE SAY USE SWEARS__
Sometimes.

__SUMMARY__
D-1221 is objectively a terrible person. Though his past is largely a mystery, we know a few things about him. His last name is 'Schieffer', and he killed his pet cat. Canonically, he dies after the events of SCP-3297 but don't let that stop you from torturing him with anything you want. Knows he's a terrible person and tried fixing it, but he fell back into old habits, ultimately landing in the Foundation as a D-Class. Idenfitifes as male.

__SUMMARY__
Faeowyn Wilson (born Felix Wilson) is Tim Wilson's daughter and the second person to run Wilson's Wildlife Solutions. She is very intelligent and inherited her father's love for animals, but is a lot more competent and responsible than him. Is a very 'no-nonsense' person. She is tan, has shoulder length hair, brown eyes, has an athletic body type, and is often seen wearing a WWS-embroidered khaki shirt and shorts. Smells of sunscreen. Is dating fellow WWS volunteer Alex Molina.
Dislikes Marshall Carter and Dark.
Is friends with Holly 'Wondertainment' Light in Wonderful World canon.
Is aware of Three Portlands and the Wanderer's Library.

Pitch black.

In front of her dad? Never. Elsewhere? depends on how angry she is.
