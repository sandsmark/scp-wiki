title:Director Ghost's Dossier
**Name:** Neil R. Ghost

**Security Clearance:** Level 4

**Location:** Sector-28 (Vancouver Island Facility), oceanic Northern Hemisphere

**Designation(s):** Safe-class SCP Maintenance Personnel, Biological/Sentient SCP Caretaker, Coastal Retrieval Director

__**Biography:**__

Author/site moderator residing in the frozen wastelands of Canadia.

Resident King of Metric, who enforces unit conversions with an iron appendage. Makes huge amounts of edits when no one's looking.

Most often seen in the IRC channel, lurking around, cracking lewd jokes, and helping new kids to avoid the banhammer.

Is generally a benevolent father figure, and calls everyone "son." All the time.

Also runs the POP QUIZ! and SCPoems pages.

__**Notes:**__

-is not, in fact, a ghost

-is, however, somewhat fat

-is not a creeper

-is usually seen wearing a toque and sun shades indoors

-is, quite possibly, a creeper
