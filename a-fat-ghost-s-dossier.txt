title:Director Ghost's Dossier
**Name:** Neil R. Ghost

**Security Clearance:** Level 4

**Location:** Sector-28 (Vancouver Island Facility), oceanic Northern Hemisphere

**Designation(s):** Safe-class SCP Maintenance Personnel, Biological/Sentient SCP Caretaker, Coastal Retrieval Director

__**Biography:**__

Site moderator residing in the lush paradise of Canadu.

Resident King of Metric, who enforces unit conversions with an iron appendage. Makes some edits when no one's looking.

Most often seen in the IRC channel, lurking around, cracking corn, and helping new kids to draft articles.

Is generally a benevolent fellow, and calls everyone "son." All the time.

__**Notes:**__

-is not, in fact, a ghost

-is, however, somewhat transparent
