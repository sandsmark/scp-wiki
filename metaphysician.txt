[[tab Dr. Lowl]]

[[include component:image-block name=http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/03/Lise_Meitner_%281878-1968%29%2C_lecturing_at_Catholic_University%2C_Washington%2C_D.C.%2C_1946.jpg/410px-Lise_Meitner_%281878-1968%29%2C_lecturing_at_Catholic_University%2C_Washington%2C_D.C.%2C_1946.jpg|caption=Dr. Judith Low, 1986.]]

**Name**: Dr. Judith Low 

**Education**: PhD in History, Master's Degree in Linguistics and Cultural Anthropology 

**Department**:  Department of History - Religious GoI Threat Analysis (semi-retired)

**Security Clearance Level**: 4

**Current Location**: Reliquary Site-26 (Gyaros, Greece)

**History**: Dr. Low was born on December 16, 1930 in Prague, Czech Republic. She was recruited by the Foundation on October 10, 1950 and was trained as part of Mobile Task Force Alpha-5 "Paranaughts" - a now disbanded MTF established during the early days of trans-dimensional exploration.

[[tab Dr. Narváez]]
 
[[include component:image-block name=http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/38/Carl-gunnar_aahlen.JPG/359px-Carl-gunnar_aahlen.JPG|caption=Dr. Calixto Narváez, 2013.]]

**Name**: Dr. Calixto Narváez

**Education**: PhD in Cognitive Psychology, Master's Degree in Philosophy

**Department**:  Department of Psychotropics/Department of Memetics 

**Security Clearance Level**: 4

**Current Location**: Area-179 (Pennsylvania, United States)

**History**: Dr. Narváez was born on August 7, 1954 in Cordoba, Spain. Recruited to the Foundation on September 12, 1982, he was assigned to SCP-2737. He would later prove instrumental in the containment of SCP-2480 and the study of SCP-2264.
