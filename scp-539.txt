    **Item #:** SCP-539

    **Object Class:** Euclid

    **Special Containment Procedures:**
    Object is to be kept locked in a padded case and will not be taken out unless a supervisor is present. Physical Contact with object is forbidden unless approved testing or usage is in progress.

    **Description:**
Object is a round disc made out of an undetermined material. When thrown, object will create a distraction of any variety that will take attention away from the thrower
